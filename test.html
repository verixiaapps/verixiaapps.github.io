Common Token Risk Signals

These are the kinds of patterns this flow is built to help people slow down and inspect before they buy.

  • Very low liquidity relative to attention and trading volume
  • Extremely fresh pair age with little trust history
  • Chaotic price action without enough structural support
  • Weak or missing project links and supporting context
  • Momentum that can pull buyers in before exits get harder

How To Use This Checker Safely

Paste the token contract address, review the selected pair carefully, then treat the result as a risk-screening step rather than a guarantee. Lower risk does not mean safe. High risk does not mean prediction. It means the available setup deserves more caution.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the token risk checker look at?

It looks at Dexscreener market context such as liquidity, volume, pair age, chain, trading activity, and available project links, then sends that context to the backend for structured AI-backed token risk analysis.

Does low risk mean a token is safe?

No. Lower risk only means fewer obvious warning signals were detected from the available market context. It does not guarantee safety, legitimacy, or future performance.

Check the structure before the story. When the setup is unclear, caution matters more than speed.
