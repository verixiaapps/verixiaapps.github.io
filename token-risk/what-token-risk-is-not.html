What Token Risk Is NOT

Before using any token risk explanation, it helps to be clear about what this type of analysis does not do.

Token risk is not price prediction.

It does not tell you where a token is going next, tomorrow, or ever.

Token risk is not buy or sell advice.

It does not recommend entering, exiting, or avoiding any token.

Token risk is not a safety guarantee.

Even low-risk tokens can fail. Risk describes structure, not outcomes.

Token risk is not a rating or score.

There are no grades, rankings, or numeric labels.

Why these limits matter

Most crypto tools fail because they promise certainty in an uncertain system.

Token Risk does the opposite: it draws clear boundaries.

The goal is understanding — not confidence, hype, or urgency.
