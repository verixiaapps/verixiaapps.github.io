FAQ
What input does Token Risk accept?
Token contract addresses only — EVM (0x...), Solana (Base58), or Tron (T...). Contract addresses give unambiguous results. Name or ticker search is not supported because DexScreener returns trading pairs, not tokens, making the result unreliable.
What does the risk level mean?
Low means liquidity is structurally stable relative to trading activity with no major ratio flags. Medium means mixed signals that warrant closer review. High means one or more serious structural problems — thin liquidity under heavy volume, extreme FDV ratio, or unknown liquidity with other concerning indicators present.
Does Token Risk give financial advice?
No. Token Risk is a structural analysis tool. It evaluates on-chain market data and returns a structural risk level. It does not say buy, sell, or avoid. All decisions remain with you.
How many checks do I get free?
One free check with no signup required. Unlimited checks are available from $3.99 per week — cancel anytime from your account.
What chains are supported?
Any chain tracked by DexScreener — Ethereum, Solana, Base, BNB Chain, Arbitrum, Polygon, Optimism, Avalanche, Tron, and more. If DexScreener has a pair for the address, Token Risk can analyze it.
