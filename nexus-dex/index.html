Frequently asked

Do I need an account to use Verixia?
No. Connect a Solana wallet — Phantom, Solflare, or Backpack — and you're in. No email, no sign-up, no KYC.
Is Verixia non-custodial?
Yes. The smart contract routes the swap directly between your wallet and the liquidity pool. Verixia never holds your funds at any point.
How fast does a swap settle?
Sub-second. Solana blocks settle in roughly 400ms at fractions of a cent in fees — no congestion, no $30 gas fees.
Which wallets are supported?
Phantom, Solflare, and Backpack are fully supported on both desktop and mobile. Any Solana-compatible wallet works.
How does best-price routing work?
Jupiter aggregates 50+ Solana DEXes — Raydium, Orca, Phoenix, and more — and splits your swap across the optimal paths to get you the best price and lowest slippage automatically.
