Nexus DEX vs Centralized Exchanges

The structural differences between self-custodial wallet trading and centralized exchange trading shape how each workflow feels in practice.

Nexus DEX

Open a perp position by signing one transaction from your Solana wallet. Margin stays in your wallet between positions.

Centralized Exchange

Open a CEX perp position by depositing funds to the exchange first, then opening leverage on their internal ledger.

Nexus DEX

How This Works

Nexus DEX perps route through on-chain perpetual futures markets. Your wallet posts USDC margin, opens a leveraged position, and settles the PnL on-chain. There is no centralized order book holding your funds.
