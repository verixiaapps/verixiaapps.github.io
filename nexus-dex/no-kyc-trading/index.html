Frequently Asked Questions

Is no-KYC trading legal?

Self-custodial wallet trading is legal in most jurisdictions. Tax and reporting obligations may still apply depending on where you live. Verixia doesn't provide tax or legal advice.

What if I lose access to my wallet?

Because Verixia is non-custodial, your wallet's seed phrase is the only recovery method. Back it up securely. Verixia cannot recover funds for you.
