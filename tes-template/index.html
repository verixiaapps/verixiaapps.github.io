How PayPal Scams Work

Scammers often send fake PayPal payment confirmations by email or text to get people to click malicious links or hand over account details.

These messages usually create urgency by claiming there is a payment issue, a security alert, or an account problem that needs immediate action.

  • Check the sender address carefully
  • Avoid clicking links inside unexpected messages
  • Log in through the official PayPal website or app to verify activity
