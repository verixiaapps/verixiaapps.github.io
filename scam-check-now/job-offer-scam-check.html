Common Job Offer Scam Signs

  • Hired instantly with no interview
  • Salary is unusually high
  • Asked to pay upfront fees
  • Only uses WhatsApp or Telegram
  • Email is Gmail or Outlook
  • Vague or copied job description

Why Job Scams Work

Scammers create urgency and excitement:

  • “We need to hire immediately”
  • “Limited positions available”
  • “High salary, no experience needed”

This pressure makes people skip verification.

Stay Safe

  • Never pay upfront
  • Never share sensitive info early
  • Always verify before accepting
