Common Job Offer Scam Signs

• Hired instantly with no interview
• Salary is unusually high
• Asked to pay upfront fees
• Recruiter only uses WhatsApp or Telegram
• Email domain does not match the company

How to Check a Job Offer

Why Job Scams Work

Scammers create urgency and excitement with messages like “We need to hire immediately” or “High salary, no experience needed.” These tactics pressure people to respond quickly without verifying the opportunity.
