How This Scam Checker Works

The AI scam checker analyzes suspicious messages, emails, job offers and websites for patterns commonly used in fraud. These include impersonation attempts, urgency tactics, suspicious domains and requests for payment or personal information.

The system evaluates the content and returns a risk level along with explanations of potential red flags and recommended next steps.

Common Scams This Tool Detects

  • Fake job offers and recruiter scams
  • Crypto investment scams and giveaways
  • Phishing emails asking for passwords
  • Marketplace and payment scams
  • Social media impersonation scams

Why People Use Scam Check Now

Online scams are becoming increasingly sophisticated. Messages may appear legitimate and often impersonate trusted companies or individuals. Checking suspicious messages before responding can help prevent financial loss and identity theft.
