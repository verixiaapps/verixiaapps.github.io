Signs a Message May Be a Scam

Many scams attempt to create urgency or pressure victims to act quickly. Messages may request payment through cryptocurrency, gift cards, or unusual transfer methods. They may also impersonate well-known companies while using suspicious links or domains.

Always be cautious if a message asks for money, login credentials, or personal information.

What To Do If You Detect a Scam

If the analysis shows a high scam risk, avoid responding to the message. Do not send money or personal information and report the message to the relevant platform or service provider.
