How Email Scams Work

Email scams often impersonate trusted companies like banks, PayPal, or delivery services. These phishing emails attempt to steal passwords, financial information, or trick victims into sending money.

Paste the email into the scam checker above to analyze common fraud signals such as suspicious links, impersonation, and payment requests.

Common Email Scam Types

• Phishing emails asking for login information
• Fake invoice or payment requests
• Cryptocurrency investment scams
• Account security alerts
• Fake job offers
