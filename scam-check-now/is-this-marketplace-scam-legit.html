About is this marketplace scam legit

Scams on online marketplaces often involve fake listings, unrealistically low prices, or sellers asking for payment outside the platform. Common warning signs include poor spelling or grammar, requests for money transfers instead of secure payments, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive a suspicious offer, do not share personal or financial information. Instead, verify the seller’s profile and reviews, use official payment methods, and report the activity to the marketplace support team. Staying cautious can help protect you from fraud and ensure a safer buying experience.

Common Scam Warning Signs

  • Requests for urgent payment or cryptocurrency
  • Messages impersonating companies or government agencies
  • Links directing you to unfamiliar websites
  • Requests for passwords, codes or personal information
  • Offers that appear too good to be true
