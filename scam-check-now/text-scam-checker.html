Common Text Message Scams

• Fake delivery notifications (USPS, FedEx, UPS)
• Bank verification or security alerts
• Cryptocurrency investment scams
• Messages asking for urgent payments
• Suspicious links requesting login details

How the SMS Scam Checker Works

Why Text Message Scams Are Increasing

Scammers often send mass SMS messages pretending to be banks, delivery companies, or employers. These messages try to create urgency or fear so recipients click malicious links or share personal information.

Checking a suspicious message before responding helps prevent phishing attacks and financial loss.
