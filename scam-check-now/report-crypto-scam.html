About report crypto scam

Crypto scams often promise quick profits through fake investments, giveaways, or urgent requests for private keys. Common signs include unsolicited messages, offers that sound too good to be true, typos, pressure to act fast, and requests for personal information or payments in cryptocurrency. If you encounter this, do not respond or send money. Instead, report it to the platform where you found it and to official authorities or consumer protection agencies. Always double-check information from trusted sources before making any transactions to stay safe.

Common Scam Warning Signs

  • Requests for urgent payment or cryptocurrency
  • Messages impersonating companies or government agencies
  • Links directing you to unfamiliar websites
  • Requests for passwords, codes or personal information
  • Offers that appear too good to be true
