Scams pretending to be from FedEx often claim there’s an issue with a package delivery, asking you to click a link or provide personal info. These messages may look official but usually contain spelling errors, urgent language, or suspicious links. Common warning signs include unexpected requests for payment, unknown sender addresses, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or share your details. Instead, visit the official FedEx website directly or contact their customer service to verify the information. Always be cautious with unsolicited communications about deliveries to protect your personal and financial information.
