Gift card scams often involve messages or calls claiming you owe money, won a prize, or must pay immediately using gift cards. Scammers ask for card numbers and PINs to steal your funds. Common warning signs include urgent demands, pressure to buy specific cards, and requests to share codes quickly. Legit businesses rarely request payment this way. If you receive such a message, don’t respond or send information. Instead, verify the claim through official channels and report the incident to local authorities or consumer protection agencies. Protect yourself by staying cautious and never sharing card details with unknown sources.
