Many scams pretending to be from PayPal involve fake emails or messages claiming there is a problem with your account or a suspicious payment. They often ask you to click a link and enter personal information. Common warning signs include poor grammar, urgent language, unfamiliar sender addresses, and links that don’t go to the official website. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or provide details. Instead, go directly to the official PayPal site and check your account for alerts. You can also forward suspicious emails to spoof@paypal.com for verification. Staying cautious helps protect your personal information and money from fraud.
