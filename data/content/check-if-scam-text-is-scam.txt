Scam messages often claim you’ve won a prize, need to verify personal info, or face urgent trouble like unpaid bills. They may use fake company names and ask for money or passwords. Common warning signs include poor spelling, suspicious links, or pressure to act quickly. If you get a suspicious message, do not reply or click links. Instead, verify the sender through official channels, report the message to your phone carrier or local authorities, and delete it. Staying cautious helps protect your personal information and avoid fraud.
