Cash app scams often involve fake messages or calls claiming you've won money, need to verify your account, or must pay fees upfront. Scammers may ask for personal info or request payment to release funds. Common warning signs include unsolicited contacts, requests for sensitive details, urgent demands for money, and links to suspicious websites. If you encounter this, do not share any personal information or send money. Instead, verify the message through official app channels and report the incident directly within the app or to the official support team. Staying cautious helps protect your funds and personal information from fraud.
