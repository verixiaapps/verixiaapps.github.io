Scammers often send fake payment notifications claiming you’ve received money, then ask for personal info or to send cash back. Common signs include requests for urgent action, unexpected payments, or messages from unknown contacts. They may also ask for details like your app login or bank info. If you get a suspicious message, do not reply or share any personal data. Instead, verify the transaction directly in your app, report the message to the official support, and block the sender. Always stay cautious and never send money or info unless you’re sure the request is legitimate.
