Many scams involve fake messages claiming to be from Zelle, asking for money or personal information. These scams often look like urgent requests to send funds or verify an account. Common warning signs include unexpected messages, spelling errors, pressure to act quickly, and requests for sensitive details. Scammers may pretend to be a bank or friend to gain trust. If you receive such a message, do not respond or send money. Instead, contact your bank directly using official contact information to verify the request. Always be cautious and never share personal or financial information with unknown sources. Reporting suspicious activity to your bank can help protect others.
