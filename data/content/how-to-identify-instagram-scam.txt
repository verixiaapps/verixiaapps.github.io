Scams on Instagram often appear as fake messages, offers, or profiles promising prizes, jobs, or discounts. They may ask for personal information, money, or prompt you to click suspicious links. Common warning signs include poor spelling, urgent requests, unfamiliar usernames, and profiles with few posts or followers. If you receive a suspicious message or encounter a doubtful account, avoid clicking links or sharing details. Report the profile or message to Instagram through the app’s reporting tools. It’s also wise to block the sender and change your password if you feel your account might be at risk. Staying cautious helps protect your personal information and keeps your online experience safe.
