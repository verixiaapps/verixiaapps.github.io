Scams involving this popular payment service often involve fake messages or calls asking users to verify their account or payment details. Scammers may claim there’s suspicious activity and request personal information or ask for money transfers. Common warning signs include unexpected contact, urgent requests, poor grammar, or links to unofficial websites. Legitimate companies rarely ask for sensitive info through messages or calls. If you receive such a contact, do not respond or click any links. Instead, verify by contacting the service directly through their official app or website. Report any suspicious activity to the platform’s support team and consider blocking the sender to protect your account and personal information.
