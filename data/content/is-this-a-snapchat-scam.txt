A common trick on Snapchat involves messages from unknown users asking for personal info, money, or to click suspicious links. These scams often promise prizes or threaten to share private content to pressure you. Warning signs include urgent requests, poor spelling, unfamiliar contacts, and links that look strange. If you get a message like this, don’t respond or click any links. Instead, block and report the sender in the app. Always verify any unexpected requests with friends or family before taking action. Staying cautious helps protect your privacy and keeps your account safe.
