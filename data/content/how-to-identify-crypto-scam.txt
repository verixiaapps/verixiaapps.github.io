Crypto scams often promise huge profits with little effort, using flashy ads or urgent messages. Common warning signs include unsolicited offers, requests for personal info or passwords, guarantees of high returns, and pressure to act quickly. Scammers may impersonate trusted figures or use fake websites. If you encounter such a message, don’t click links or share sensitive details. Verify the source through official channels and report suspicious activity to platform support or authorities. Staying cautious helps protect your money and personal data from fraud.
