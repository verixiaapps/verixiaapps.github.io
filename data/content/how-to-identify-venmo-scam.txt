Scams on Venmo often involve fake requests for money, payment confirmations for items never received, or overpayments asking for refunds. Common warning signs include messages from unknown users, urgent requests to send money quickly, and deals that seem too good to be true. Scammers may also impersonate friends or businesses, asking to settle payments outside Venmo or through other methods. If you receive a suspicious message, do not send money or share personal information. Verify the request by contacting the person directly through a trusted method. Report the suspicious activity to Venmo immediately and consider blocking the sender to protect your account. Staying cautious helps keep your transactions safe.
