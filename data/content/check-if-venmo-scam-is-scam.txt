A common scam on Venmo involves messages or requests from strangers asking for money, often with urgent or emotional stories. Scammers may send fake payment notifications or ask for refunds on payments you never made. Warning signs include unknown contacts, requests for personal information, unusual payment amounts, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive a suspicious request, don’t respond or send money. Verify the sender’s identity by contacting them through a different method. Report the incident to Venmo through their app or website and consider notifying your bank or financial institution. Staying cautious and double-checking requests can help protect you from falling victim to scams.
