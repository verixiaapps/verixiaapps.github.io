Fake job offers often promise high pay with little effort and may come from unofficial email addresses. Common warning signs include requests for personal information upfront, asking for money to start, vague job descriptions, and poor grammar. Scammers might pressure you to act quickly or offer unrealistic benefits. If you receive such an offer, research the company using official websites, contact them directly to verify, and never share sensitive details or send money. Reporting suspicious messages to job platforms or authorities helps protect others. Stay cautious and trust your instincts when something feels off.
