Scams in the crypto space often promise quick profits or free coins in exchange for a small payment or personal information. They may come as messages, emails, or social media posts claiming to be from trusted sources. Common warning signs include requests for private keys, urgent pressure to act, offers that sound too good to be true, and suspicious links. If you receive such an offer, do not share your wallet details or send money. Instead, verify the message through official channels and report it to relevant authorities or platform support. Staying cautious helps protect your assets and personal information from fraud.
