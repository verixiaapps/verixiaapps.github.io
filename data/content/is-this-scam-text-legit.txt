Scam messages often claim you won a prize, owe money, or need to verify personal details. They may come from unknown numbers, use urgent language, or include suspicious links. Common warning signs include poor spelling, requests for sensitive information, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive such a text, avoid clicking links or sharing personal data. Instead, verify the message by contacting the company directly using official contact details. Reporting the text to your phone carrier or relevant authorities can help protect others from fraud. Staying cautious and informed is key to avoiding scams.
