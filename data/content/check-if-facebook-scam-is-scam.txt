Scams on Facebook often appear as messages or posts offering prizes, urgent requests for money, or suspicious links. They may promise free gifts, ask for personal information, or claim to be from a friend in need. Common warning signs include poor grammar, unfamiliar senders, unrealistic offers, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive such a message, avoid clicking links or sharing details. Verify the sender’s identity by contacting them directly through a different method. Report the suspicious content to Facebook using their reporting tools to help protect others. Always stay cautious and trust your instincts when something feels off online.
