Scammers often pretend to be from a well-known package delivery company, claiming there’s a problem with your shipment or payment. They may ask for personal information, payment, or direct you to click on suspicious links. Common warning signs include unexpected messages, urgent requests for money or details, poor spelling, and unfamiliar phone numbers or email addresses. If you receive such contact, do not respond or click links. Instead, check your shipment status directly through the official website or app. Report suspicious messages to the delivery company and your local authorities to stay safe from fraud.
