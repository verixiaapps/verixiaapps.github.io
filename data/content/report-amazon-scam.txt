Amazon scams often come as fake emails, messages, or calls pretending to be from Amazon. These scams may claim you owe money, won a prize, or need to verify your account. Common warning signs include poor spelling, urgent requests, suspicious links, and unknown sender addresses. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or provide personal information. Instead, go directly to the official Amazon website or app to check your account. Report the suspicious contact to Amazon through their official help page to protect yourself and others from fraud. Always stay cautious and verify before acting on any unexpected communication.
