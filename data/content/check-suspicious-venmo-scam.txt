Scams on Venmo often involve fake payment requests, offers to send money in exchange for goods, or messages asking for personal information. Common warning signs include unexpected requests from strangers, offers that seem too good to be true, or links asking for login details. If you receive a suspicious message, don’t click any links or share your personal info. Verify the sender by contacting them through a trusted method. Report the activity to Venmo immediately through their app or website. Always keep your account secure by using strong passwords and enabling two-factor authentication. Staying cautious helps protect your money and personal data from fraud.
