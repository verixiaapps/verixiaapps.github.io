A romance scam often starts with someone online pretending to be interested in a relationship. They may quickly express strong feelings and ask for personal information or money. Common warning signs include requests for cash, reluctance to meet in person, inconsistent stories, and pressure to keep communication secret. Scammers often create fake profiles with stolen photos. If you suspect a scam, stop all contact immediately. Do not share personal details or send money. Report the suspicious account to the platform and consider informing local authorities or consumer protection agencies. Staying cautious and verifying the person’s identity can help protect you from harm.
