This scam often involves a stranger asking for money through a payment app, claiming urgent reasons like emergencies or refunds. They may pressure you to pay quickly or send a fake payment confirmation. Common warning signs include unsolicited requests, unusual stories, or payments from unknown people. If you receive such a message, do not send money or share personal details. Verify the request by contacting the person directly using trusted methods. Report suspicious activity to the payment service and local authorities. Staying cautious helps protect you from losing money to fraud.
