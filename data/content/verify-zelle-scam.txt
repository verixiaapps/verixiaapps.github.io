Scammers using Zelle often pretend to be someone you trust or a company, asking for quick payments. They might say it’s urgent, like a bill or a prize claim. Common warning signs include requests for money from unknown people, pressure to pay immediately, or asking for personal info. If you get a suspicious message or payment request, don’t respond or send money right away. Instead, contact the person or company directly using official contact details. Report the incident to your bank and Zelle support to protect your account and help stop fraud. Always be cautious with unexpected payment requests.
