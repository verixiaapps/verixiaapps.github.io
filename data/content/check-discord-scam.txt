Scams on Discord often appear as messages from fake friends, bots, or accounts offering free gifts, game codes, or asking for personal info. Common warning signs include unexpected links, poor grammar, urgent requests, and offers that seem too good to be true. Scammers may try to steal your password, money, or personal data. If you receive a suspicious message, do not click any links or share your details. Instead, report the user to Discord and block them. Always verify with trusted sources before responding, and enable two-factor authentication to protect your account. Staying cautious helps keep your online experience safe.
