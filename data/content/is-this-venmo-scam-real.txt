This scam often involves a message claiming you've received money, asking you to click a link or confirm details. It may look like it’s from a trusted source but is actually fake. Common warning signs include unexpected payment notifications, poor grammar, urgent language, and links asking for personal info. If you get one, don’t click any links or share your details. Instead, verify the transaction directly in the official app or website. Always be cautious with unexpected messages about money to protect your account and personal information. If unsure, contact the payment service’s support team for help.
