Marketplace scams often involve fake listings or sellers offering goods at prices that seem too good to be true. Scams typically include requests for payment outside secure platforms, such as wire transfers or gift cards. Common warning signs are unusually low prices, vague product descriptions, pressure to act quickly, or sellers unwilling to meet in person. If you encounter a suspicious offer, do not share personal or payment information. Instead, report the listing to the platform and contact customer support. Always use secure payment methods and verify the seller’s reputation before making a purchase to protect yourself.
