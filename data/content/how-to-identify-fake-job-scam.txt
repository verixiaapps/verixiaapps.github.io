Fake job scams often appear as attractive offers with high pay and little work. They may come via email, social media, or fake websites. Common warning signs include requests for upfront fees, personal information, or bank details early on. Poor grammar, vague job descriptions, and pressure to act quickly are also red flags. If you receive such an offer, do not share personal data or send money. Instead, research the company, verify contact details, and check reviews. Report suspicious messages to job platforms or local authorities to protect yourself and others from fraud.
