Scammers on WhatsApp often send messages pretending to be someone you know or official companies. These messages may ask for personal information, money, or ask you to click suspicious links. Common warning signs include unexpected requests, poor spelling or grammar, urgent language pressuring you to act quickly, and messages from unknown numbers. If you receive such a message, do not respond or click any links. Verify the sender by contacting them through a trusted method. Report the message to WhatsApp and block the number to protect yourself and others. Staying cautious helps keep your information safe and prevents falling victim to fraud.
