A common scam involves someone sending a fake payment notification claiming you received money through Venmo. The scammer then asks you to send the funds elsewhere or provide personal information. Warning signs include unexpected payment messages, requests for immediate action, or links to suspicious websites. Legitimate transactions will always appear in your official app with full details. If you receive such a message, do not respond or click any links. Instead, check your Venmo account directly through the app or official website to verify payments. Report any suspicious activity to Venmo support and avoid sharing personal or financial information. Staying cautious helps protect you from losing money to fraud.
