Cash App scams often involve messages or requests asking for money, personal details, or login information. Scammers may pose as friends, support staff, or sellers offering deals too good to be true. Common warning signs include urgent requests for payment, suspicious links, unexpected contacts, and poor grammar or spelling. If you receive such a message, avoid clicking links or sharing personal info. Instead, verify the sender’s identity through official channels. Report suspicious activity directly to the app’s support team and consider blocking the user. Staying cautious can protect your money and personal data from fraud.
