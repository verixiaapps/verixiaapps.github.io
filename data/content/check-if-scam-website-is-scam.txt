Scam websites often mimic real sites, offering fake deals or asking for personal info. They may look professional but contain errors, unusual URLs, or urgent messages. Common warning signs include poor spelling, suspicious links, requests for payment details, and offers that seem too good to be true. If you encounter such a site, avoid clicking on links or sharing any personal information. Instead, verify the website’s legitimacy by checking reviews, using online tools, or contacting the company directly. Reporting the site to authorities or your browser can also help protect others. Stay cautious and trust your instincts when something feels off online.
