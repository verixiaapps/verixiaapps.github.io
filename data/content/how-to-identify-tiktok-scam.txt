Scams on TikTok often appear as messages promising free gifts, prizes, or easy money in exchange for personal information or payments. Common warning signs include urgent requests, suspicious links, unfamiliar usernames, and offers that seem too good to be true. Scammers may also ask you to share the message with friends or download unknown apps. If you receive such messages, avoid clicking links or sharing details. Report the account or message to TikTok immediately and block the sender. Always verify offers through official channels to stay safe.
