This type of scam often involves someone sending a message claiming they need money urgently or offering a deal that sounds too good to be true. They may ask you to send funds using Zelle quickly. Common warning signs include pressure to pay immediately, requests from unknown contacts, or messages with spelling errors and unusual language. If you receive such a request, do not send any money. Verify the person’s identity by contacting them through a trusted method. Report the suspicious activity to your bank and the Zelle support team. Always be cautious with unexpected payment requests to protect yourself from fraud.
