Scam sites often mimic real businesses or appear overly attractive with fake offers. They may ask for personal info like passwords or payment details. Common warning signs include poor spelling, unusual web addresses, urgent requests, and unverified contact info. If you encounter one, avoid clicking links or sharing details. Instead, close the site immediately and report it to your email provider or a trusted authority. Always double-check website URLs and look for secure connections (https://) before entering any information. Staying cautious helps protect your data and finances.
