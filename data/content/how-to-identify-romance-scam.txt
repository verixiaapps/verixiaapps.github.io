Romance scams often start with someone online expressing strong feelings quickly and asking to continue communication privately. They may share a flattering story or claim to be working abroad. Common warning signs include requests for money, inconsistent details about their life, reluctance to meet in person, or pressure to keep things secret. If you notice these behaviors, trust your instincts and avoid sending money or personal information. Always verify the person’s identity through video calls or mutual contacts. If you suspect a scam, stop all contact and report it to the platform you’re using or to local authorities to protect yourself and others from harm.
