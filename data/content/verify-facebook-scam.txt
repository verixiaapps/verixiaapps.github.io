Scammers on Facebook often pose as friends, companies, or trusted sources to trick users. Common signs include urgent messages asking for money or personal info, suspicious links, and offers that sound too good to be true. Poor grammar and unfamiliar profiles can also be clues. If you get a suspicious message, don’t click links or share details. Instead, verify by contacting the person directly or checking official pages. Report the message to Facebook to help protect others. Staying cautious and double-checking requests can keep you safe from online fraud.
