A common scam involves fake payment notifications from popular money apps, tricking people into thinking they received money. Scammers often ask for personal info or prompt users to send funds back or elsewhere. Warning signs include unexpected messages claiming you’ve been paid, requests for personal details, or urgency to act quickly. If you get a suspicious message, don’t respond or click any links. Verify transactions directly in your official app, and never share sensitive information. Report the message to the app’s support team and consider alerting local authorities if you’ve shared personal info or money. Staying cautious helps protect your finances from fraud.
