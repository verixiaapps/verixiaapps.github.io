These scams often appear as messages promising prizes, job offers, or exclusive deals on TikTok. They usually ask for personal information, upfront payments, or require clicking suspicious links. Common warning signs include poor spelling, urgent requests, unsolicited contact, and offers that sound too good to be true. If you receive such a message, do not share any personal details or send money. Instead, block the sender and report the account to TikTok. Always verify offers through official channels before taking action to protect your privacy and avoid potential fraud.
