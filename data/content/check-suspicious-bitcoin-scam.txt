Suspicious messages about Bitcoin scams often promise huge profits, ask for upfront payments, or pressure you to act quickly. Common warning signs include unsolicited offers, requests for personal information, poor grammar, and links to unknown websites. Scammers may impersonate trusted companies or celebrities to gain your trust. If you receive such a message, avoid clicking links or sharing details. Instead, verify the sender’s identity through official channels and report the incident to relevant authorities or the platform where you encountered it. Staying cautious helps protect your money and personal information from fraud.
