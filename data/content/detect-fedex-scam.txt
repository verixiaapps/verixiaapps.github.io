Scammers often send fake messages claiming to be from FedEx, saying there’s a package delivery problem or a missed shipment. These messages may ask for personal details, payment, or to click on suspicious links. Common warning signs include poor spelling or grammar, unfamiliar sender addresses, urgent language pressuring immediate action, and unexpected delivery notifications. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or provide information. Instead, visit the official FedEx website or contact their customer service directly using verified contact details to confirm any delivery issues. Staying cautious helps protect your personal data and prevents falling victim to fraud.
