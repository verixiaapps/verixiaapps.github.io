Marketplace scams often involve fake listings or sellers asking for payment outside the platform. Scammers may offer items at unusually low prices or pressure buyers to act quickly. Common warning signs include requests to pay via wire transfer or gift cards, poor grammar, and no verifiable contact information. If you encounter suspicious offers, avoid sharing personal or financial details. Always use secure payment methods provided by the platform and verify the seller’s profile. Report any suspicious activity to the marketplace support team immediately to help protect yourself and others. Staying cautious can prevent losing money or falling victim to fraud.
