Scams involving popular payment apps often start with messages asking for money or personal info. Typically, the scammer pretends to be a friend, official, or seller, requesting payment through the app. Common warning signs include urgent requests, unfamiliar contacts, promises of free money, or links asking for login details. If you receive such a message, do not click links or share your password. Verify the sender by contacting them directly using known information. Report suspicious activity to the app’s support team immediately to protect your account and personal data. Staying cautious helps prevent falling victim to these types of fraud.
