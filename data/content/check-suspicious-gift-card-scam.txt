A common scam involves someone asking for payment via gift cards. Typically, the scammer pretends to be a trusted person or authority, urging quick payment for a fake emergency or bill. Warning signs include pressure to act immediately, requests for gift card numbers or codes, and unusual payment methods. Scammers often say the card is the only way to pay or keep the request secret. If you receive such a message, don’t share any gift card details. Instead, verify the request by contacting the person or company directly using official contacts. Report the suspicious attempt to your local authorities or consumer protection agency to help prevent others from falling victim.
