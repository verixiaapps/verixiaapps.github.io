Scams involving popular payment apps often trick users with fake offers or payment requests. Typically, scammers claim to send money or ask for a small fee to release a larger amount. Warning signs include unsolicited messages, requests for personal info or upfront payments, and links that look suspicious. These fraudsters may also impersonate friends or official support teams. If you receive such a message, do not respond or share any details. Instead, verify the sender’s identity through official channels and report the incident to the app’s support service. Always keep your account info private and be cautious of deals that seem too good to be true. Staying alert helps protect your money and personal data.
