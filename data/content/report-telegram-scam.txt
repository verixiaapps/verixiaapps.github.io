Scams on Telegram often involve messages from fake accounts pretending to be trusted contacts, companies, or officials. They may ask for money, personal details, or direct you to suspicious links. Common warning signs include urgent requests for cash, messages from unknown numbers, spelling errors, and links asking for login information. If you receive such a message, do not reply or click any links. Instead, block the sender and report the conversation within the app. Always verify information through official channels before taking any action. Staying cautious helps protect your personal data and money from fraudsters.
