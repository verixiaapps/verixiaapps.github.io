This type of scam often involves fake messages claiming to be from Amazon, alerting you about orders, payments, or account issues. They may ask for personal information or prompt you to click suspicious links. Common warning signs include poor grammar, urgent language, unfamiliar sender addresses, and requests for sensitive details. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or provide information. Instead, check your official Amazon account directly or contact Amazon customer service through their official website. Always verify messages before taking action to protect your personal and financial information.
