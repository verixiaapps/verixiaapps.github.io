Telegram scams often involve messages promising quick money, prize wins, or fake job offers to trick users into sharing personal info or sending money. Common warning signs include unsolicited contact, requests for payments or sensitive data, urgent language, and suspicious links. The sender may impersonate trusted organizations or contacts. If you receive such a message, do not respond, avoid clicking links, and never share personal or financial details. Report the user to Telegram and block them. Always verify offers through official channels before taking action to protect yourself from fraud.
