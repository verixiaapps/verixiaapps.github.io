A romance scam often starts with someone pretending to be interested in a relationship, usually online. They build trust quickly and may share a fake story to gain sympathy. Common warning signs include requests for money, inconsistent details in their stories, and avoiding face-to-face meetings or video calls. If you receive messages like this, don’t send any money or personal information. Instead, stop communication and report the person to the platform you met them on. Staying cautious and trusting your instincts can help protect you from falling victim to these dishonest schemes.
