A marketplace scam often involves fake listings or offers that seem too good to be true, such as unusually low prices or sellers asking for payment outside the platform. Common warning signs include poor grammar, pressure to pay quickly, requests for wire transfers or gift cards, and lack of verified seller information. If you encounter a suspicious offer, do not send money or share personal details. Instead, report the seller to the marketplace support team and check reviews or feedback from other buyers. Always use secure payment methods provided by the platform to protect yourself from fraud. Staying cautious helps keep your online shopping safe.
