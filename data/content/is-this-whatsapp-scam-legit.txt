Many people receive messages claiming to be from WhatsApp with promises like free subscriptions or prize winnings. These scams often ask you to click links, share personal information, or forward the message to others. Common warning signs include urgent language, suspicious links, requests for passwords or verification codes, and poor spelling or grammar. Legitimate companies rarely ask for sensitive details through messages. If you get such a message, avoid clicking any links or sharing information. Instead, delete it and verify the claim through official sources or the app itself. Reporting the message to WhatsApp can also help protect others from falling victim. Staying cautious keeps your account and data safe.
