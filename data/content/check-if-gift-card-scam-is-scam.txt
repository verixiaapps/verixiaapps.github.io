Gift card scams often involve a caller or message claiming you owe money or must pay immediately using gift cards. Scammers may pretend to be from a government agency, tech support, or a loved one in trouble. Common warning signs include urgent demands for payment, secrecy, and requests to buy specific gift cards or share the card numbers. Legitimate organizations rarely ask for payment this way. If you receive such a request, do not provide any gift card details or payments. Instead, hang up or ignore the message, and report it to local authorities or the platform where you encountered the scam. Always verify suspicious claims independently before taking action.
