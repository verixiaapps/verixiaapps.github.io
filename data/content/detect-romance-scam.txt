Romance scams often involve someone pretending to be interested in a relationship to gain trust, then asking for money or personal information. These scammers may use fake profiles and quickly express strong emotions. Common warning signs include requests for money, inconsistent stories, refusal to meet in person, and poor grammar or spelling. If you suspect a scam, avoid sharing personal details, do not send money, and block the person. Report the incident to the website or platform where you met, and consider informing local authorities. Staying cautious can protect you from emotional and financial harm.
