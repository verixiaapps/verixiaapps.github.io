Scams on TikTok often involve fake messages claiming you’ve won a prize or need to verify your account details. These scams may ask for personal information, passwords, or payments. Common warning signs include poor grammar, urgent requests, suspicious links, and unfamiliar senders. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or share your information. Instead, verify the claim through official channels like TikTok’s website or app. Report the message using TikTok’s reporting tools to help protect yourself and others from fraud. Always stay cautious when interacting with unexpected messages or offers online.
