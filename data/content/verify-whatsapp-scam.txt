Scammers often send fake messages pretending to be from WhatsApp, offering prizes or asking for personal information. These messages may claim your account is at risk or that you won a reward, urging you to click links or share details. Common warning signs include poor spelling, urgent language, unknown senders, and requests for passwords or codes. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or share personal info. Instead, verify the message by checking official WhatsApp channels or contacting their support. Always keep your app updated and use two-step verification to protect your account from fraud.
