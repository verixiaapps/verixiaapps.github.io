A common scam involves messages claiming you have a check or money waiting for you. These texts often ask for personal information or request payment to release funds. Warning signs include urgent language, unfamiliar senders, spelling errors, and offers that seem too good to be true. Scammers may also ask for your bank details or ask you to cash a fake check and send money. If you receive such a message, do not respond or click any links. Avoid sharing personal information and never send money. Instead, delete the message and report it to your phone carrier or a trusted authority to help protect yourself and others from fraud.
