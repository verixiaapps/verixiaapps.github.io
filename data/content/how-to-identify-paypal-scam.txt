A common PayPal scam involves fake emails or messages claiming there is an issue with your account or a payment problem. Scammers often ask for personal information or direct you to a fake login page. Warning signs include poor spelling, urgent language, unfamiliar sender addresses, and links that don’t match PayPal’s official website. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or provide details. Instead, log in to your account directly through the official site to check for alerts. Report suspicious messages to PayPal and delete them. Staying cautious helps protect your money and personal information from fraud.
