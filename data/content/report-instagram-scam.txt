Scams on Instagram often appear as messages from fake accounts pretending to be friends, celebrities, or official brands. They may ask for personal information, money, or prompt users to click suspicious links. Common warning signs include poor grammar, urgent requests, unfamiliar profiles, and offers that seem too good to be true. If you receive such a message, avoid responding or clicking any links. Instead, block the sender and use Instagram’s built-in tools to report the suspicious activity. Protecting your account and staying cautious helps keep your online experience safe.
