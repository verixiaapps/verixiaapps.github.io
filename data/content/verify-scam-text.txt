Scam messages often claim you've won a prize, need to confirm personal details, or have an urgent problem with your bank or account. They usually come from unknown numbers or suspicious addresses. Common warning signs include poor spelling or grammar, requests for personal information, unsolicited links, and a sense of urgency to act quickly. If you receive one, do not click any links or reply. Instead, verify the message by contacting the company or person directly through official channels. You can also report the message to your phone carrier or relevant authorities to help protect others. Staying cautious helps keep your information safe.
