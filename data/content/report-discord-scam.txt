Scams on Discord often involve messages promising free gifts, game credits, or account boosts to trick users into sharing personal information or clicking harmful links. Common warning signs include urgent requests, unfamiliar links, poor grammar, or offers that seem too good to be true. If you encounter such messages, do not click any links or share details. Instead, block the sender and report the suspicious activity through Discord's built-in reporting tools. Staying cautious and verifying offers before trusting them helps keep your account safe from fraud.
