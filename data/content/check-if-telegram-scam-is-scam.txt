Telegram scams often involve messages promising easy money, fake giveaways, or urgent requests for personal information. Scammers may impersonate friends, celebrities, or official accounts to gain trust. Common warning signs include unsolicited messages, spelling errors, requests for money or account details, and links that lead to suspicious websites. If you receive such messages, do not click on any links or share personal information. Verify the sender’s identity through a different channel, and report the message to Telegram. Staying cautious helps protect your privacy and avoid fraud.
