Many scams on social media pretend to offer prizes, jobs, or deals but aim to steal personal info or money. They often look like urgent messages from friends or official pages, asking for clicks, downloads, or payments. Common warning signs include poor spelling, requests for money or personal details, unfamiliar links, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive a suspicious message, avoid clicking links or sharing info. Verify the sender by contacting them directly through a trusted method. Report the message to the platform and delete it. Staying cautious helps protect your data and prevent fraud.
