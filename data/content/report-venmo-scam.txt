A common Venmo scam involves fraudsters asking for money through fake requests or pretending to be someone you know. They might claim you owe them or offer deals that seem too good to be true. Warning signs include unsolicited payment requests, urgent messages pressuring you to pay quickly, and links or codes asking for personal info. If you receive such a message, do not respond or send money. Instead, verify the request with the person directly using a different contact method. Report suspicious activity to Venmo through their official app or website, and consider contacting your bank to protect your account. Staying cautious helps keep your money and information safe.
