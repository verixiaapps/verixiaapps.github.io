Loan scams often promise easy money with little to no credit checks. They usually ask for upfront fees or personal information before approving any funds. Common warning signs include requests for payment upfront, unrealistic approval promises, unsolicited offers, and poor grammar or spelling in messages. If you receive such an offer, do not share personal details or send money. Instead, research the lender’s legitimacy, check for reviews online, and contact your bank or a trusted financial advisor. Reporting the suspicious activity to consumer protection agencies can help prevent others from being targeted. Always verify before committing to any financial agreement.
