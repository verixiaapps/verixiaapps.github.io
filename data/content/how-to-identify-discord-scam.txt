Scams on Discord often appear as messages from fake accounts offering free gifts, asking for personal information, or urging you to click suspicious links. Common warning signs include poor spelling and grammar, urgent requests, unknown senders, and offers that seem too good to be true. Scammers may also impersonate friends or official servers to gain trust. If you receive such messages, do not click any links or share personal details. Instead, report the user to Discord’s moderation team and block them immediately. Always verify offers through official channels and be cautious with unexpected messages to protect your account and privacy.
