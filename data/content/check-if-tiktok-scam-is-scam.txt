Some scams on TikTok promise easy money, free gifts, or exclusive deals but are designed to trick users. They often ask for personal information, payments upfront, or to click suspicious links. Common warning signs include unrealistic offers, poor grammar, pressure to act quickly, and requests for private data. If you receive such messages or see suspicious accounts, don’t share your details or send money. Instead, report the content or user to TikTok, delete the message, and consider blocking the sender. Staying cautious helps protect your personal info and prevents falling victim to fraud.
