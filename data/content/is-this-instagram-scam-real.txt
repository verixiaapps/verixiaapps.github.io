This type of scam often appears as a message or comment claiming you’ve won a prize, need to verify your account, or asking for personal information. Common warning signs include urgent language, poor spelling, unfamiliar links, or requests for money or passwords. Scammers may impersonate Instagram or influencers to seem trustworthy. If you get a suspicious message, don’t click any links or share details. Instead, check the sender’s profile carefully and report the message to Instagram. Always use official app features to verify notifications. Staying cautious helps protect your account and personal data from fraud.
