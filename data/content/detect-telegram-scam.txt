Scams on messaging apps often involve fake offers, urgent requests, or suspicious links. Scammers may pose as friends, companies, or officials to gain trust. Common warning signs include messages with poor grammar, unexpected requests for money or personal information, and pressure to act quickly. Links leading to unknown websites or offers that seem too good to be true are red flags. If you receive a suspicious message, do not click on any links or share personal details. Verify the sender’s identity through a different channel and report the message to the app’s support team. Stay cautious to protect your information and avoid falling victim to fraud.
