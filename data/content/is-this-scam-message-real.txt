Scam messages often claim you’ve won a prize, need to verify personal info, or face urgent problems like account suspension. They may come from unknown numbers or look like official companies but contain spelling errors or strange links. Common warning signs include requests for money, sensitive data, or clicking suspicious links. If you receive such a message, don’t respond or share any details. Instead, verify the information by contacting the company directly using official contact methods. You can also report the message to your phone provider or local authorities. Staying cautious helps protect your personal information and prevents falling victim to fraud.
