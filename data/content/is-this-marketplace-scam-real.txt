This scam often appears as fake listings or offers on popular buying and selling platforms. Scammers may ask for payment outside the official system or pressure you to act quickly. Common warning signs include unusually low prices, requests for wire transfers or gift cards, poor grammar in messages, and sellers unwilling to meet in person or provide detailed information. If you encounter this, avoid sending money or sharing personal details. Report the suspicious activity to the marketplace’s support team and consider informing local authorities. Always use secure payment methods and verify the seller’s identity before making a purchase to stay safe.
