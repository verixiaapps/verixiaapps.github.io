This scam often appears as a direct message from someone pretending to be a Discord staff member or a trusted friend, offering free Nitro or account help. It usually contains suspicious links or asks for personal information. Common warning signs include unexpected messages, urgent language pushing you to click links, and requests for your password or verification codes. Never share your login details or click on unknown links. If you receive such a message, do not respond or follow any instructions. Instead, report the user to Discord through their official channels, block the sender, and enable two-factor authentication on your account to stay safe.
