Scams on online marketplaces often involve fake listings, overly low prices, or sellers asking for payment outside the platform. Common warning signs include poorly written descriptions, requests for unusual payment methods, and pressure to act quickly. If a deal sounds too good to be true, it likely is. To protect yourself, never share personal or financial information outside the official site, verify seller reviews, and use secure payment options provided by the platform. If you encounter suspicious activity, report it to the marketplace support team immediately to help prevent fraud and keep the community safe.
