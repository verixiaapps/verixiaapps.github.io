A romance scam often involves someone pretending to be interested in a romantic relationship to gain trust and money. They usually create fake profiles, quickly express strong feelings, and ask for financial help due to emergencies or travel costs. Common warning signs include inconsistent stories, reluctance to meet in person, poor grammar, and requests for money or personal information. If you suspect this type of fraud, stop communication immediately, avoid sending money, and report the situation to the platform and local authorities. Staying cautious and verifying the person’s identity can help protect you from falling victim to this kind of deception.
