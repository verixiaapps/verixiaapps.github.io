A common fake message on Snapchat often promises free gifts, money, or exclusive content in exchange for personal info or clicking a link. These scams usually look urgent and too good to be true. Warning signs include poor grammar, unknown senders, suspicious links, and requests for passwords or payment details. If you receive such a message, don’t click any links or share your information. Instead, block the sender and report the message to Snapchat through the app’s reporting tools. Staying cautious helps protect your privacy and account security.
