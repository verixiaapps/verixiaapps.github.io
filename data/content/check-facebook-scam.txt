Scams on social media often appear as messages offering deals, prizes, or urgent requests from friends. They may include suspicious links or ask for personal information. Common warning signs are poor grammar, unfamiliar senders, too-good-to-be-true offers, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive such a message, avoid clicking links or sharing details. Instead, verify the sender’s identity by contacting them through a different channel. Report the suspicious content to the platform to help protect others. Staying cautious and informed helps keep your information safe online.
