Scams on Instagram often appear as fake messages, comments, or offers that seem too good to be true, such as giveaways, urgent requests for money, or links asking for personal details. Common warning signs include poor spelling or grammar, unfamiliar accounts with few followers, urgent language pushing for quick action, and suspicious links. If you receive a suspicious message or offer, do not click on links or share personal information. Instead, report the account to Instagram, block the sender, and inform friends or family to stay cautious. Always verify offers directly through official sources to protect yourself from fraud.
