Many scams on WhatsApp involve messages that ask you to click links, share personal info, or send money urgently. They often claim to be from a trusted source like a friend, bank, or the app itself. Common warning signs include unexpected requests, poor spelling or grammar, pressure to act quickly, and unfamiliar phone numbers. If you receive a suspicious message, do not click any links or share your details. Instead, verify the sender by contacting them directly using a known phone number. You can also report the message to WhatsApp and block the sender to stay safe. Always stay cautious to protect your personal information and devices.
