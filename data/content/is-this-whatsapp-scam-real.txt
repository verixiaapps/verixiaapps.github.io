This scam often appears as a message claiming you've won a prize, received a gift, or need to verify your account. It may ask you to click a link or share personal information. Common warning signs include poor spelling, urgent language, unknown senders, and requests for money or passwords. If you get a suspicious message, do not click any links or share details. Instead, delete the message and block the sender. You can also report it to the app’s support team. Always verify such claims through official channels to protect your personal data and avoid becoming a victim.
