Scammers often pretend to be FedEx, sending fake messages about packages or deliveries. These messages may ask for personal information, payment, or prompt you to click suspicious links. Common warning signs include poor spelling or grammar, urgent language demanding immediate action, unknown senders, and unexpected fees. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or provide information. Instead, verify the delivery status directly on the official FedEx website using your tracking number. You can also contact FedEx customer service to confirm. Report the suspicious message to FedEx and your local authorities to help protect others from fraud. Staying cautious helps keep your information safe.
