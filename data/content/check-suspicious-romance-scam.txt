Romance scams often begin with someone showing strong interest quickly, usually online. They may use flattering messages and share emotional stories to gain trust. Common warning signs include requests for money, reluctance to meet in person, inconsistent details, and avoiding video calls. If you receive such contact, do not send money or personal information. Verify their identity through video chats or by searching their details online. Report suspicious activity to the platform where you met them and consider informing local authorities. Staying cautious helps protect your heart and finances from fraud.
