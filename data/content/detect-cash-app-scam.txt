Scammers often target users with fake messages or requests involving money transfers, claiming you owe money or won a prize. These scams usually ask for personal information or prompt urgent payments. Common warning signs include unsolicited messages, requests to send money quickly, unfamiliar links, and poor spelling or grammar. If you receive such a message, don’t reply or click any links. Verify the sender’s identity through official channels, and report suspicious activity to the app’s support team. Always protect your personal information and never share passwords or verification codes. Staying cautious helps keep your finances safe.
