Fake job offers often promise high pay for little work and arrive via email or social media. Scammers may ask for personal information or money upfront, claiming it’s for training or equipment. Common warning signs include inconsistent company details, poor grammar, unrealistic salaries, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive such an offer, research the company thoroughly and contact them directly through official channels. Avoid sharing personal data or sending money before verifying. Reporting suspicious offers to job sites or authorities can help protect others from falling victim. Staying cautious can prevent scams and keep your information safe.
