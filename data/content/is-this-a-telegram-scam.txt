This scam often involves messages from unknown contacts claiming prizes, urgent requests for money, or links to suspicious websites. Common warning signs include poor grammar, unsolicited offers that seem too good to be true, and pressure to act quickly. Be cautious if the message asks for personal information or payment upfront. If you receive such a message, do not click on links or share details. Instead, block the sender and report the conversation through the app’s security features. Always verify any unexpected claims through official sources before responding. Staying alert helps protect your personal information and prevents falling victim to fraud.
