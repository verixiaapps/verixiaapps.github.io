A romance scam often involves someone pretending to be interested in a romantic relationship to gain trust and money. Scammers usually create fake profiles with attractive photos and sweet messages. Common warning signs include requests for money, vague or inconsistent personal details, and a rush to move the conversation off the original platform. If you suspect a scam, avoid sending money or personal information. Try to verify the person’s identity by video calling or checking their online presence. Report suspicious behavior to the website or app support team and consider blocking the contact. Staying cautious can protect you from emotional and financial harm.
