Instagram scams often appear as messages or comments claiming you've won a prize, asking for personal details, or offering fake verification badges. Common warning signs include urgent requests for passwords, links to suspicious websites, poor spelling, and profiles with few followers or posts. Scammers may impersonate official accounts to gain trust. If you receive such messages, do not click on links or share any personal information. Instead, report the account to Instagram and block the sender. Always verify offers by visiting official sources directly, and enable two-factor authentication to protect your account. Staying cautious helps keep your information safe.
