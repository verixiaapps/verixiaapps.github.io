Many scams on Snapchat involve fake messages claiming you've won a prize or need to verify your account. Scammers often ask for personal info, payment, or direct you to suspicious links. Common warning signs include urgent requests, poor grammar, unknown contacts, and offers that seem too good to be true. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or share personal details. Instead, verify the sender’s identity through official channels, report the message to Snapchat, and block the user. Staying cautious helps protect your account and personal information from fraud.
