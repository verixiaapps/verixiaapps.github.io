Scams on social media often appear as messages or posts promising easy money, prizes, or urgent help requests. They may come from fake profiles or hacked accounts. Common warning signs include poor grammar, requests for personal information, suspicious links, and offers that seem too good to be true. If you receive such a message, avoid clicking any links or sharing details. Instead, verify the sender’s identity through a separate, trusted contact method. Report the suspicious content to the platform immediately to help protect yourself and others. Staying cautious and skeptical can prevent falling victim to these fraud attempts.
