Suspicious messages on Telegram often claim you’ve won prizes, offer deals, or ask for personal info. They may come from unknown contacts or fake profiles pretending to be friends or official accounts. Common warning signs include urgent requests for money or codes, poor grammar, and links asking for login details. If you get such a message, do not click any links or share your information. Verify the sender’s identity through other means and report the conversation to Telegram. Stay cautious and avoid responding to unexpected requests to protect your privacy and accounts.
