A common scam involves asking for payment using gift cards. Scammers often pretend to be from trusted organizations, like government agencies or tech support, and demand gift card codes as payment. Warning signs include urgent demands, requests for secrecy, and refusal to provide official information. If you receive such a message or call, do not share any codes. Instead, hang up and verify the request independently by contacting the company or agency directly. Report the incident to local authorities or consumer protection agencies. Staying cautious helps protect your money and personal information from fraud.
