Scams on WhatsApp often appear as urgent messages asking for money, personal information, or verification codes. They may claim to be from friends, family, or official organizations. Common warning signs include poor spelling, unexpected requests, unfamiliar links, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive such a message, avoid clicking links or sharing details. Verify the sender by contacting them through a different method. Report the message within the app and block the sender to protect yourself. Staying cautious helps prevent falling victim to these scams.
