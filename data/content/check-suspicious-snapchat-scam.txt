Scammers on Snapchat often send messages or friend requests pretending to be someone you know or a celebrity. They may ask for money, personal information, or urge you to click suspicious links. Common warning signs include unexpected messages from unknown accounts, urgent requests for money, poor spelling or grammar, and links that look unusual. If you receive such messages, do not respond or click any links. Instead, block the sender and report the account to Snapchat. Always verify requests by contacting the person directly through another platform. Staying cautious helps protect your information and keeps your account safe.
