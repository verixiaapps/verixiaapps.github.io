Gift card scams often involve someone asking you to pay with gift cards for goods, services, or to clear debts. Scammers may pretend to be from a trusted company, government agency, or family member in trouble. Common warning signs include urgent demands for payment, requests to keep it secret, and pressure to act quickly. Legitimate organizations rarely ask for gift cards as payment. If you receive such a request, do not share any card numbers or codes. Instead, verify the situation by contacting the person or company directly using official contact information. Report suspicious messages to the platform or authorities to help prevent others from falling victim. Stay cautious and protect your money.
