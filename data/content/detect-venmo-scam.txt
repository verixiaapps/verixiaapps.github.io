Scammers on Venmo often send fake payment requests or messages pretending to be friends or buyers. They may ask for personal information or payment outside the app. Common warning signs include unexpected requests, pressure to act quickly, offers that seem too good to be true, and links asking for login details. If you receive a suspicious message, don’t click any links. Verify the sender by contacting them directly outside Venmo. Avoid sharing personal or financial information. Report the suspicious activity to Venmo immediately through the app or website to help protect yourself and others from fraud. Always use Venmo’s official channels for transactions to stay safe.
