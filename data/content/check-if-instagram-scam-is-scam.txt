Scams on Instagram often appear as messages or posts promising free gifts, giveaways, or asking for personal information. They may use fake profiles that look like celebrities or trusted brands. Common warning signs include poor grammar, urgent requests for money or login details, and links to suspicious websites. If you receive such messages, do not click on any links or share personal information. Instead, verify the sender’s profile carefully and report the content to Instagram. Always be cautious of offers that seem too good to be true and protect your account by using strong passwords and two-factor authentication.
