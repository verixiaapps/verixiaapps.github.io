Scam texts often appear as urgent messages claiming you’ve won a prize, need to verify account details, or face an emergency. They may include suspicious links or ask for personal information. Common warning signs include poor spelling or grammar, unfamiliar senders, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or provide personal details. Instead, delete the text and report it to your mobile carrier or a trusted authority. Staying cautious helps protect your personal information and prevents financial loss.
