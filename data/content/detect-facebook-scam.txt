Facebook scams often appear as fake messages or posts offering prizes, urgent help requests, or suspicious links. Common warning signs include poor spelling, promises that seem too good to be true, unexpected contact from strangers, and requests for personal information or money. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or share your details. Instead, verify the sender’s identity through other means, report the content to Facebook using their reporting tools, and delete the message. Staying cautious helps protect your privacy and prevents falling victim to fraud.
