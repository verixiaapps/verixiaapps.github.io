Scams on Discord often involve messages from fake accounts pretending to be friends or official sources. They may ask for personal information, offer too-good-to-be-true deals, or share suspicious links. Common warning signs include unexpected requests for money, poor grammar, urgent messages pressuring you to act fast, and usernames that look similar but are slightly different from real ones. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or share personal details. Instead, report the user to Discord, block them, and inform trusted friends or moderators. Staying cautious helps protect your account and personal information from fraud.
