Scams involving peer-to-peer payment apps often involve strangers asking for money or sending fake payment confirmations. A typical trick is someone claiming to send you money but later reversing the payment after you’ve shipped goods or provided services. Common warning signs include requests for payment from unverified accounts, urgent pressure to send money quickly, or messages with poor grammar. If you receive a suspicious message, do not send any money until you verify the transaction through the app itself. Contact the official customer support to report the issue and consider blocking the sender. Always double-check payments before completing any deal to protect yourself from fraud.
