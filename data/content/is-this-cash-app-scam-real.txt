This type of scam often appears as messages or emails claiming you’ve received money or a prize via Cash App, urging you to click a link or share personal info. Common warning signs include unsolicited offers, requests for your login details, or messages with poor grammar and urgent language. Scammers try to steal your account or money by pretending to be from the official service. If you get such a message, do not click links or share data. Instead, verify the claim directly through the official app or website. Report suspicious activity to Cash App support and delete the message to protect yourself from fraud.
