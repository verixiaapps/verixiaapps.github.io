Scam sites often mimic legitimate ones but aim to steal personal information or money. They may have poor design, spelling mistakes, or unusual web addresses. Common warning signs include requests for sensitive data, offers that seem too good to be true, and lack of contact information. If you encounter a suspicious site, do not enter any personal details. Instead, close the page and run a security scan on your device. Report the site to relevant authorities or your browser to help protect others. Always double-check website URLs and use trusted sources when shopping or sharing information online.
