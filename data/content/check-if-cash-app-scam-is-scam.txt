Scammers often impersonate Cash App support or users, asking for personal info, payments, or promising quick money. Common warning signs include unsolicited messages, requests for sensitive details, fake payment screenshots, and links to suspicious websites. They may pressure you to act fast or offer deals that seem too good to be true. If you receive such a message, do not respond or click on any links. Instead, verify the source by contacting the official Cash App support directly through the app or website. Report the suspicious activity to Cash App and consider alerting your bank or financial institution to protect your account. Staying cautious helps keep your money and information safe.
