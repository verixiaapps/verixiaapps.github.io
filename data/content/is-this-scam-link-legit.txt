Scam links often appear as urgent messages claiming you've won a prize or need to verify your account. They may look official but usually contain spelling mistakes or suspicious web addresses. Common warning signs include unexpected requests for personal information, offers that seem too good to be true, and links from unknown sources. If you receive a suspicious link, avoid clicking it. Instead, verify the message through official company websites or contact customer support directly. Always keep your security software updated and report the message to help protect others from potential fraud. Staying cautious can prevent identity theft and financial loss.
