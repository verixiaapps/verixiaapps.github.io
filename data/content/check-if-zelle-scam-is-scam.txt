Zelle scams often involve messages or calls asking for money transfers, claiming urgent reasons like emergencies or prizes. Scammers may pose as friends, family, or trusted companies. Common warning signs include requests for immediate payment, pressure to use Zelle or similar apps, and unfamiliar or suspicious contacts. If you receive such a message, avoid sending money or sharing personal information. Always verify the request by contacting the person or company directly through official channels. If you suspect fraud, report it to your bank and local authorities immediately to protect your finances and identity.
