Loan scams often appear as offers promising easy approval or unusually low interest rates. They may ask for upfront fees or personal information before giving any money. Common warning signs include pressure to act quickly, requests for payment via untraceable methods, or unsolicited contact from unknown lenders. If you receive such an offer, avoid sharing personal details or sending money. Instead, verify the lender’s credibility through official channels and report suspicious activity to consumer protection agencies. Staying cautious helps protect your finances and personal information from fraud.
