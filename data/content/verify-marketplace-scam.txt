Marketplace scams often involve fake sellers or buyers trying to steal money or personal information. Scams may look like offers that seem too good to be true, requests for payment outside the platform, or messages asking for sensitive details. Common warning signs include prices that are much lower than usual, urgent requests to pay via wire transfer or gift cards, and poor spelling or grammar. If you encounter suspicious activity, do not share personal information or send money. Report the user and conversation to the marketplace support team immediately. Always use the platform’s official payment methods and verify the other party’s profile before making a deal. Staying cautious helps protect you from fraud.
