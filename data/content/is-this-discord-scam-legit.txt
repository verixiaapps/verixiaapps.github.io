This scam often involves messages on Discord that promise free items, gifts, or account boosts in exchange for personal information or clicks on suspicious links. Common warning signs include urgent language, requests for passwords or verification codes, unknown senders, and poor spelling or grammar. Scammers may also impersonate friends or official accounts to appear trustworthy. If you receive such a message, avoid clicking links or sharing details. Always verify with the person or organization directly through official channels. Report the message to Discord and block the sender to protect yourself and others from potential harm. Stay cautious and never share sensitive information online.
