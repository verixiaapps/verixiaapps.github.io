Scams on messaging apps often involve fake messages claiming prizes, urgent requests for money, or offers that seem too good to be true. Common warning signs include unknown contacts asking for personal details, links leading to suspicious websites, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive such a message, do not click on any links or share your information. Instead, verify the sender’s identity through official channels and report the message within the app. Staying cautious and double-checking suspicious requests helps protect your personal data and avoid financial loss.
