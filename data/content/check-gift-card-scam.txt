A common fraud involves scammers asking for gift cards as payment. They may claim you owe money, won a prize, or face an emergency. Usually, they pressure you to buy cards quickly and share the codes. Warning signs include urgent demands, requests for payment via gift cards only, and threats if you don’t comply. Legitimate businesses or government agencies do not ask for gift cards as payment. If you get such a request, do not send any information or money. Instead, hang up or ignore the message. Report the incident to local authorities or consumer protection agencies to help stop these scams and protect others.
