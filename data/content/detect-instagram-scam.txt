Scams on Instagram often appear as messages or posts promising quick money, free gifts, or fake giveaways. They may ask for personal information, payment, or login details. Common warning signs include unknown senders, urgent requests, suspicious links, poor grammar, and offers that seem too good to be true. Always verify the sender’s profile and avoid clicking on unexpected links. If you receive a suspicious message, do not respond or share personal details. Instead, report the account to Instagram and block the user to protect yourself and others. Staying cautious and informed helps keep your online experience safe.
