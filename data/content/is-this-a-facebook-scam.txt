Scams on social media often come as friend requests, messages, or posts promising prizes, free offers, or urgent help. They may use fake profiles or impersonate someone you know. Common warning signs include poor spelling, requests for money or personal information, suspicious links, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive something that feels off, don’t click any links or share your details. Instead, verify the sender’s identity through another channel, report the message to the platform, and delete it. Staying cautious helps protect your personal information and keeps your account safe.
