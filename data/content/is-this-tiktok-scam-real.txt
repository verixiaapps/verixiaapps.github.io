This scam often appears as messages or videos promising free gifts, money, or exclusive offers if you click a link or share personal info. Common warning signs include urgent requests, spelling mistakes, unfamiliar links, and offers that seem too good to be true. Scammers may also ask for passwords, payment details, or direct you to suspicious websites. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or provide information. Instead, block the sender, report the content to the platform, and verify offers through official sources. Staying cautious helps protect your personal data and prevents falling victim to fraud.
