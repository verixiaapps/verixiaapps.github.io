Scam messages often claim you’ve won a prize, need to verify personal information, or face urgent issues with your bank or account. They usually have spelling errors, strange links, or ask for money or passwords. Common warning signs include unexpected requests, poor grammar, and pressure to act quickly. If you receive one, do not click on links or reply. Instead, delete the message and report it to your phone carrier or relevant authorities. Always verify any claims by contacting the company or person directly using official contact details. Staying cautious helps protect your personal information and avoid falling victim to fraud.
