Marketplace scams often involve fake listings or sellers asking for payment outside secure platforms. Scammers may offer items at unusually low prices or request quick payment through wire transfer or gift cards. Common warning signs include suspiciously low prices, pressure to act fast, poor grammar in messages, and requests to pay outside the official system. If you encounter such a situation, do not send money or share personal information. Instead, report the suspicious activity to the marketplace’s support team immediately. Also, block the user and avoid further contact. Staying cautious helps protect your money and personal data from fraud.
