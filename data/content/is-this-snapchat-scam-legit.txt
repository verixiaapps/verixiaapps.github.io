Many scams on Snapchat involve messages or friend requests from strangers claiming to offer money, gifts, or links to "exclusive" content. These scams often ask users to click suspicious links or share personal information. Common warning signs include unexpected messages from unknown accounts, urgent requests for money or login details, and poorly written text. If you receive such messages, avoid clicking on any links and do not share personal information. It’s best to block and report the sender through the app. Always verify suspicious offers by checking with trusted sources before taking any action to protect your account and privacy.
