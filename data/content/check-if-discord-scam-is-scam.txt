Scams on Discord often involve messages or links promising free gifts, Nitro codes, or account boosts. These usually look urgent, asking you to click a link or share personal info. Common warning signs include poor grammar, unfamiliar senders, unexpected requests for passwords, and links leading to non-official websites. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or provide personal details. Instead, report the user to Discord and block them. Always verify offers through official Discord channels and keep your account secure by enabling two-factor authentication. Staying cautious helps protect you from falling victim to these common tricks.
