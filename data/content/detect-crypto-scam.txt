Crypto scams often promise easy money or high returns quickly. They may appear as fake investment opportunities, phishing emails, or social media ads claiming to double your coins. Common warning signs include unsolicited messages, requests for personal information or private keys, offers that sound too good to be true, and pressure to act fast. If you encounter such a situation, avoid clicking on links or sharing sensitive details. Verify the source independently, and report suspicious activity to relevant platforms or authorities. Staying cautious helps protect your digital assets and personal information.
