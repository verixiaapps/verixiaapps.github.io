A romance scam often involves someone pretending to be interested in a relationship online, quickly expressing strong feelings. They usually ask for money, gifts, or personal information. Common warning signs include requests for cash, inconsistent stories, avoiding in-person meetings, and pressure to act fast. If you receive such messages, don’t send money or share personal details. Instead, stop communication, report the person to the platform, and inform local authorities if money was lost. Trust your instincts and stay cautious when interacting with strangers online.
