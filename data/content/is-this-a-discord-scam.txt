These scams often involve messages from someone pretending to be a friend or a trusted source asking for personal information, login details, or money. They may offer fake giveaways or claim your account is at risk. Common warning signs include urgent requests, suspicious links, poor spelling, and offers that seem too good to be true. If you receive a message like this, do not click links or share any personal info. Verify the sender’s identity by contacting them through another method. Report the message to Discord’s trust and safety team and enable two-factor authentication to protect your account. Staying cautious helps keep your account safe.
