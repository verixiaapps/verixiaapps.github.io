These scams often appear as messages from fake accounts promising prizes, giveaways, or exclusive deals. They may ask for personal information, payment, or to click suspicious links. Common warning signs include poor spelling, urgent language, unexpected requests, and profiles with few followers or posts. If you receive such a message, avoid sharing any details or clicking links. Verify the offer by checking the official page or contacting the company directly through trusted channels. Report the suspicious account to Instagram to help protect others. Always stay cautious and remember that legitimate companies rarely ask for sensitive information via direct messages.
