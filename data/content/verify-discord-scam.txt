Scammers often send fake messages pretending to be from Discord, asking users to verify their account by clicking links or sharing personal info. These messages usually promise rewards or threaten bans to create urgency. Common warning signs include poor spelling, suspicious links, requests for passwords or codes, and messages from unknown users. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or share your details. Instead, report the message to Discord through their official support and block the sender. Always verify any claims by checking Discord’s official website or help center to stay safe from fraud.
