This scam often appears as a message or post promising free gifts, money, or exclusive offers. It may ask you to click a link, share personal information, or forward the message to friends. Common warning signs include poor grammar, urgent language, requests for sensitive details, and suspicious links. The sender could be pretending to be a friend or a well-known company. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or provide personal information. Instead, verify the offer through official channels and report the message to Facebook. Staying cautious helps protect your account and privacy from fraud.
