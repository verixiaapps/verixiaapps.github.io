Suspicious offers on online marketplaces often promise great deals but may be scams. These usually involve sellers asking for payment outside the platform, offering unusually low prices, or providing vague product details. Common warning signs include poor seller ratings, pressure to act quickly, and requests for payment via wire transfer or gift cards. If you encounter a questionable listing or message, do not share personal information or send money. Instead, report the suspicious activity to the marketplace’s support team and verify the seller’s credibility through reviews and official channels. Staying cautious helps protect you from fraud and ensures a safer shopping experience.
