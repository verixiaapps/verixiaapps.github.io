A common trick involves someone asking you to send gift card numbers as payment or to verify a prize. Scammers often pretend to be officials, tech support, or relatives in urgent trouble. Warning signs include pressure to act quickly, requests for unusual payment methods, or threats if you don’t comply. Legitimate organizations rarely ask for gift cards this way. If you get such a request, don’t share any codes or personal info. Instead, contact the company or person directly using official contact details. Report the incident to local authorities or consumer protection agencies to help prevent others from being targeted. Staying cautious helps keep your money and information safe.
