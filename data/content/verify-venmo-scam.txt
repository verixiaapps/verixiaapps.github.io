A common scam involves fake payment notifications claiming you received money through Venmo. Scammers often ask you to “verify” the payment by clicking a link or sharing personal details. Warning signs include unexpected messages, requests for personal info, urgent language, and links to unofficial sites. If you get such a message, don’t click any links or provide information. Instead, check your Venmo account directly via the official app or website to confirm any transactions. Report suspicious messages to Venmo’s support team and delete them. Staying cautious helps protect your money and personal data from fraud.
