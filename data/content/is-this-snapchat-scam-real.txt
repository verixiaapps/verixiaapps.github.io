Many scams on Snapchat involve fake messages from people pretending to be friends or popular accounts, often asking for money or personal info. They might send links that lead to harmful websites or ask for login details. Common warning signs include urgent requests, unusual language, links you weren't expecting, and messages from unknown or new contacts. If you receive something suspicious, don’t click links or share personal details. Instead, block the sender and report the message to Snapchat. Always verify with the person directly through another method if you’re unsure. Staying cautious helps protect your account and personal information.
