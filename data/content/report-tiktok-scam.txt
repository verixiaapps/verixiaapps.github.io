Scams on TikTok often involve fake giveaways, suspicious links, or requests for personal information. They may promise rewards or ask you to send money to unlock prizes. Common warning signs include poor spelling, urgent messages, unknown contacts, and requests to share private details or payment. If you encounter such messages, do not click on any links or share information. Instead, report the suspicious account or content directly through the app’s reporting features. Block the sender to prevent further contact and consider informing friends to stay safe. Staying alert can protect you from fraud and keeps your online experience secure.
