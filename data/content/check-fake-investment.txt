Scams often promise high returns with little or no risk, using flashy websites or urgent messages. They may ask for personal details or upfront payments quickly. Common warning signs include guarantees of big profits, pressure to act fast, unclear how the investment works, and unlicensed sellers. If you receive such an offer, pause and research thoroughly. Check official financial regulators for any complaints or warnings. Never share personal or bank information without verifying the source. When in doubt, consult a trusted financial advisor before making decisions. Protecting yourself with careful checks can prevent losses and stress.
