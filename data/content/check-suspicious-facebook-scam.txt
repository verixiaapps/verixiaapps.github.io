Scams on social media often appear as messages or posts offering prizes, urgent requests for help, or links to suspicious websites. Common warning signs include poor spelling or grammar, unsolicited contact from unknown accounts, and offers that seem too good to be true. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or share personal information. Instead, verify the sender’s identity through other means, report the content to the platform, and delete the message. Staying cautious can protect your personal data and prevent falling victim to fraud.
