Scammers on WhatsApp often send messages claiming you’ve won a prize, need to verify your account, or must click a suspicious link. These messages may contain urgent language or ask for personal details and money. Common warning signs include unfamiliar contacts, poor grammar, unexpected links, and requests for sensitive information. If you receive such a message, do not respond, click any links, or share personal data. Instead, block the sender and report the chat within the app to help protect yourself and others. Always verify information with trusted sources before taking action. Staying cautious can keep your account and data safe.
