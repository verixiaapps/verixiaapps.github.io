scams on Instagram often appear as direct messages or comments from fake profiles offering prizes, jobs, or deals that seem too good to be true. Common warning signs include poor grammar, urgent requests for personal information, suspicious links, and profiles with few followers or posts. If you receive such a message, avoid clicking on any links or sharing your details. Instead, report the account to Instagram and block the sender.
