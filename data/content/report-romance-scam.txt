A romance scam typically involves someone pretending to be interested in a relationship to gain trust and then ask for money or personal information. These scammers often use fake profiles and heartfelt messages. Warning signs include quickly declaring strong feelings, asking for financial help, avoiding in-person meetings, and inconsistent stories. If you receive such messages, do not share personal details or send money. Report the situation to the platform where you met the person and consider contacting local authorities or consumer protection agencies. Staying cautious and verifying identities can help protect you from falling victim to this type of fraud.
