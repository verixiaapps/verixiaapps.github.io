Scams involving Ethereum often promise quick profits, free tokens, or exclusive giveaways to trick you into sending money or sharing private info. Common warning signs include unsolicited messages, unrealistic offers, requests for private keys or passwords, and links to fake websites. If you receive such offers, never share your wallet details or send funds without verifying the source. Instead, research the sender, check official channels, and use trusted platforms. When in doubt, ignore the message and report it to the relevant authorities or community forums to protect yourself and others.
