A common scam involves fake emails or messages claiming to be from PayPal, often asking for personal information or payment. These messages may look urgent, stating your account is limited or a payment is pending. Warning signs include poor spelling, suspicious links, requests for passwords or financial details, and unfamiliar sender addresses. If you receive such a message, do not click any links or provide information. Instead, log in directly to your official account through the PayPal website or app to check for alerts. You can also report the suspicious message to PayPal by forwarding it to their official fraud email. Staying cautious helps protect your account and personal data.
