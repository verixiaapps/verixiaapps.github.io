WhatsApp scams often appear as messages claiming you’ve won a prize, urgent requests for money, or suspicious links. Common warning signs include poor spelling, unfamiliar senders, pressure to act quickly, and requests for personal information or payments. These scams try to trick you into giving away data or money. If you receive a suspicious message, do not click any links or share details. Instead, verify the sender’s identity through other means, block the contact, and report the message within the app. Staying cautious helps protect your personal information and prevents fraud.
