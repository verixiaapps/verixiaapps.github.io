Frequently asked questions

Do I need to create an account or complete KYC to use Verixia?
No. Verixia is DeFi -- non-custodial, on-chain, no account creation of any kind. No email, no password, no KYC, no liveness check, no phone number. Connect your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, or compatible), review the quote, and execute. Your wallet holds the keys throughout. Disconnect anytime and no ongoing permissions remain.
How does Verixia get me the best price?
Verixia checks liquidity across every major Solana DEX in parallel -- Raydium, Orca, Meteora, Phoenix, Lifinity, OpenBook, and others -- and routes your trade through whichever combination of pools produces the highest net output. Larger trades often get split across multiple pools so price impact stays low. The whole quote refreshes every block, and the route is shown before you sign.
What does "non-custodial" actually mean?
Your private keys never leave your wallet. Funds move directly between wallets through the smart contract -- Verixia never holds, custodies, or has authority to move your tokens at any point. The trade-off: you are responsible for your own seed phrase and wallet security.
Which Solana wallets does Verixia support?
Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, Glow, and every major Solana wallet that supports the standard wallet adapter. Hardware wallets like Ledger work through Phantom or Solflare's hardware integration. Connect, sign, swap -- no app store install required, browser-based.
What chains does Verixia support?
Solana only. Full SPL token support across Raydium, Orca, Meteora, Phoenix, Lifinity, OpenBook, and every other major Solana DEX. One chain, every pool, best price.
