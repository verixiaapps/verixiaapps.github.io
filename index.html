What Scam Check Now Looks For
Every check scans for the fraud patterns that cost people the most money.
🚨
Urgency & Pressure Tactics
Scams push you to act before you think. Countdown timers, "act now or lose access," and time-limited offers are primary manipulation tools.
🎭
Impersonation
Fake banks, government agencies, delivery services, tech support, and trusted brands. The sender looks real. The destination is not.
🔗
Suspicious Links & Domains
Misspelled domains, URL shorteners hiding destinations, lookalike sites, and pages asking for credentials before they identify themselves.
💸
Unusual Payment Requests
Gift cards, wire transfers, crypto, Zelle to strangers. Legitimate organizations do not ask for payment this way.
